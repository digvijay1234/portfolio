Project

Website & Menu design.

Objective

To create Website & Menu design for a Japanese based Ramen shop called Kame Ramen Ha.

Tools Used

The Brief

The client envisioned a modern, sleek web experience that captured the warmth and detail of traditional Japanese ramen culture. The solution needed to support both in-store browsing and online ordering, catering to customers who value design as much as flavor.

Design Approach

Crisp. Intentional.

We focused on simplicity and flow. The layout uses negative space generously to highlight high-res food images, bold typography for clarity, and subtle animations for microinteractions.

  • Color Palette: Muted creams, charcoal black, and hints of deep red — grounded in Japanese minimalism.
  • Typography: A mix of modern sans-serif with traditional brushstroke accents for category headers.
  • Structure: Easy scroll, clear menu sections (Ramen, Sides, Drinks), and prominent CTA buttons for action.

Outcome

The prototype received strong positive feedback for its balance of elegance and usability. It’s not just a menu—it’s an invitation into the ramen shop’s atmosphere. Whether you’re browsing from your phone or sitting down at the table with a digital menu, the experience remains consistent and delightful.

Conclusion

This project was more than just designing a menu—it was about capturing the soul of a ramen shop and translating it into an immersive digital experience. By blending thoughtful UX with clean, purposeful design, we created a platform that not only informs but invites. The result is a menu and website that feel as handcrafted as the ramen it celebrates.
